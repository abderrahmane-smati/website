Museum of the Future

As a senior in-house designer, I played a key role in shaping the Museum of the Future’s visual identity, crafting impactful designs for print, digital screens, exhibitions, and marketing campaigns. I developed engaging visuals for events, workshops, and social media, ensuring every design resonated with audiences. A highlight was leading the creative direction for the museum’s collaboration with SXSW in Austin, Texas, requiring a full event rebrand across digital and print platforms. My work merged innovation, storytelling, and strategy, transforming complex ideas into compelling visuals that inspired visitors and positioned the museum as a global creative force.
